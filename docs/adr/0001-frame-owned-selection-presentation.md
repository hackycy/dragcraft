# Frame-owned selection presentation

Selected-node geometry is a Renderer-owned projection, while its visual presenter is replaceable and mounts into content or viewport coordinate planes supplied by the container shell. A root segment preserves its material's complete border box; the default presenter paints its top and bottom edges outside the material and lets the frame provide the inline overlap needed to cover frame borders. Device frames therefore make their presentation planes span the frame border box, measure complete chrome wrappers, and reserve block gutters between viewport edges, chrome, and scrolling content so exterior selection edges remain visible without overlap; native scrolling and overflow still clip complete projections without visible-intersection calculations, and invalid overflow inside material containers remains outside the framework contract.
